About Me

Mrs. Hawaa'

Position: Senior Librarian

Hello! I'm Mrs. Hawaa', a university librarian with over 10 years of experience supporting students and faculty in academic research, referencing, and information literacy.

I'm passionate about helping learners find the right resources, navigate digital databases, and build strong research skills.

Whether you're writing your first research paper or completing your thesis, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

Throughout my career, I've conducted countless workshops on citation management, database searching, and avoiding plagiarism. I'm also an advocate for open educational resources and digital literacy initiatives.

Beyond the library, I enjoy reading about educational technology and mentoring student researchers. Feel free to reach out for one-on-one consultations or collaborative research support.

Vision & Mission

Our core values that guide our services and support.

Vision

To be the leading source of research support, empowering every learner to access, evaluate, and create knowledge for the advancement of society.

Mission

  • Provide personalized assistance to students, researchers, and faculty.
  • Promote information literacy and critical thinking skills.
  • Facilitate access to quality academic resources and databases.
  • Support lifelong learning and scholarly success through library services.
  • Foster a welcoming and inclusive research environment for all users.
